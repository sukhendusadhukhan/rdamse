## 📌 About RDAMSE
**RDAMSE (Recent Developments and Applications in Materials Science and Engineering)** is an international conference that brings together researchers, academicians, scientists, and industry professionals to share knowledge and innovations in materials science.

---

## 🎯 Objective
- Provide a global platform for research collaboration  
- Promote innovation in materials science & engineering  
- Encourage interaction between academia and industry  
- Address real-world challenges:
  - Clean Energy  
  - Sustainable Development  
  - Environmental Issues
